The Turnstile Encounter, navigable

A schematic 3D reconstruction of the Oslo Freeport break-in — built to explore its spatial and temporal logic. The Protagonist fights his own inverted self without knowing it.

  • A hijacked Boeing 747 crashes into the freeport as a distraction. The Protagonist and Neil enter through the crash hole.
  • They discover the Rotas turnstile — a time-inversion machine hidden in the freeport vault. FORWARD Protagonist enters from the east; INVERTED Protagonist (his future self) emerges from the turnstile moving backward through time.
  • The inverted self bursts through a glass wall. They fight in the corridor — the forward self doesn't realize he's fighting himself. Bullets un-fire; movements reverse.
  • The inverted self retreats back toward the turnstile. Neil pulls the Protagonist out as the freeport burns.
  • Switch views (left) to ride with the forward or inverted Protagonist, or with Neil. The subjective timeline for the inverted self runs the clock backward.
