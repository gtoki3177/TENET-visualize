Interface Prototype · v3 · Battle of Stalsk-12

The Temporal Pincer, navigable

A schematic 3D reconstruction of the final assault — built to explore its spatial and temporal logic, not to reproduce film footage. Geometry is stylised.

  • FORWARD Red Team moves with the clock. INVERTED Blue Team moves against it — reverse-playing as you scrub.
  • The field runs on a south→north spine: main LZ + arches (south) → turnstile → battlefield entrance → battlefield buildings → double-exploding building (the 5:00 mark) → tunnel entrance (north). Red Team inserts at the southern LZ; both teams later extract at the SE hypocenter. Helicopters carrying a container are extracting (troops out); bare ones are inserting. The splinter unit — only the Protagonist & Ives — is rope-lifted out of the hypocenter as the bomb blows, with Neil driving in.
  • Switch views (left) to ride with a team or character; jump to locations (right) — LZ, turnstile, tunnel entrance, vault, hypocenter.
  • Hit X-RAY (top-right) to make the ground transparent and trace the highlighted U-turn tunnel running underground from the northern entrance to the vault beneath the SE hypocenter.
  • Neil crosses time more than once, so the same Neil co-exists on the field as up to three selves you can ride with: Neil — FWD (turns red when he reverts at the turnstile and drives in to rescue them), Neil — BWD his inverted (blue) copy on the battlefield, and Neil — After (blue) — the later self who goes back in to lock the gate and take the bullet.
